About GPA Calculator

WHAT IS CGPA & GPA??

CGPA - Cumulative Grade Point Average. (This is what you have after both 1st and 2nd semester .... In summation and Division, amounting to it)

GPA - Grade Point Average ( This is what you have after every semester)

  1. C.U - Credit Units
  2. TCU - Total Credit Unit
  3. G.P - Grade Point
  4. TGP or TQP - Total Grade Point or Quality Point
  5. Thus; GPA ..... This is derived by dividing the Total Grade/Quality Point (TQP/TGP) by the Total Credit Unit (TCU) for that semester.

For example: If in a semester, a student earned 90 quality points for 21 credit units, the GPA will be; GPA = TGP/TCU 90/21 = 4.28

How is the GPA calculated?

CGPA is derived by adding the Total Quality Points (TQP) for all the semesters to date and dividing by the Total Credit Units to date for all semester... 1st & 2nd.

For example: If in a semester a student earns a QP of 90 for 21 CU in the first semester and earns a QP of 87 for 20 CU in the second semester, His/Her CGPA will be ; CGPA = TQP/TCU = (90+87) / (21+20) = 177 / 41 = 4.31 > ° ° ° Your CGPA for that session will be, CGPA = 4.31
